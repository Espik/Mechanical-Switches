On the Subject of Mechanical Switches

Tic Tac TAK TAK TAK

On the module there are five computer keyboard keys that may produce different sounds.

Use Table 2 and find which rule is true first. Then use the five values to the right of the rule and hold the keys until they glow its color, in the order based on the color of the switches.

Some keys may contain the same switch.

If you hold the wrong key, you will see its correct color, but you will also receive a strike and the module will reset and the keys will change.

Keys are numbered based on the order in Table 1 from top to bottom then left to right.
